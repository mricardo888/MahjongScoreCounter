# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 01, 2026

Mahjong Score Counter ("the App") is designed to be a simple, offline utility for tracking Mahjong scores. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how data is handled.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

- **Offline Operation**: The App operates entirely on your device. It does not require an internet connection and does not have the `INTERNET` permission.
- **Local Data Storage**: All game data (scores, round history) and settings (theme preferences) are stored locally on your device using Android's DataStore mechanism. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to any external servers.
- **Third-Party Access**: Since no data is collected or transmitted, no third parties have access to your information through this App.

## 2. Permissions

The App uses minimal permissions required for its core functionality:
- **Internal Storage**: Used solely to save your current game state and preferences so they persist when you close and reopen the App.

## 3. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App does not connect to the internet, we recommend checking this document occasionally for any updates.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the app store listing or project repository.
